You should work with a partner on this exercise. Interview your partner to find out a few things about them — including their first name, their hometown, and their favorite food. You can add more things about your partner as well, if you'd like, but make sure to have those three at a minimum.

# 1. Create some variables.

      - firstName

      - hometown

      - favoriteFood

# 2. Use those variables to write a short bio.

Using string concatenation and the variables you created in step 1, make a short 1-2 sentence bio. The result should be assigned to a variable named bio.

# 3. Print the bio variable to the console.

For example:

> "Louis is a carefree dude hailing from Atlanta, Georgia. When he's home he loves to go to his favorite diner for biscuits and gravy."

Using the above as an example, firstName would have been assigned to"Louis", hometown to "Atlanta, Georgia", and favoriteFood to "biscuits and gravy". Be sure to include spaces and periods where necessary!
